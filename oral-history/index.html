The Center for Public History is a New York organization dedicated to the development of history that is accessible to everyone. Our goal is to promote awareness of issues in the museum, archives and education fields and to provide resources that help professionals in those fields reach and interact with the public more effectively. We are strongly committed to the openness of information and reducing barriers of entry to those who wish to access or promote history.
